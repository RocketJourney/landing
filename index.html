It's a game

RocketJourney is a game of consistency. The goal is to sustain an exercise streak for as many weeks in a row as possible.

RocketJourney is more fun and engaging when played in teams.

Let your members team up, compete, and motivate each other along their Journey.

Show off your brand

RocketJourney is open to the world, so your members can team up with their friends, even if they exercise at home or outdoors.
